Brownies

One of my favs, it goes well with ice cream, smoothies and more. Its a popular dessert.

List of Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Nutella
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Put flour inside a bowl
  2. Crack 3 eggs
  3. Pour 250g of nutella in the bowl
  4. Mix it
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking pan
  6. Bake at 175 degres celsius for 15 minutes
  7. Serve with chilled drink. Enjoy!

Pasta

List of Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Veggies e.g Carrot, Cabbage, Spring Onions, Onions
  • Groundnut oil
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Put water on fire and pour your pasta inside it
  2. Add groundut oil and salt to taste
  3. Add your pepper
  4. Add your seasoning
  5. Add your diced veggies
  6. Allow it to cook for 10 to 15 minutes
  7. Serve with Chicken/Meat etc and chilled drink. Enjoy!

Puff puff

List of Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pepper(optional)
  • Groundnut oil to fry

Steps:

  1. Make a lukewarm water
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, pinch of salt and yeast in the water
  3. Add the grated pepper to the mixture(optional)
  4. Leave it in a warm place to rise for 10 minutes
  5. Heat your oil
  6. Now put balls of the mixture into the hot oil
  7. Remove on fire when it turns golden or light brown
  8. Serve with chilled drinks. Enjoy!

Eggs & Plantain

List of Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Plantains
  • Pepper/Onions
  • Seasoning
  • Groundnut oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Break the eggs into a plate
  2. Add your salt, seasoning into the mixture
  3. Heat the groundnut oil
  4. Slice the pepper and onions and stir fry in the hot oil
  5. Pour your egg mixture into the oil, stir fry until its golden or light brown
  6. Slice the Plaintain into biteable sizes
  7. Heat the groundnut oil and fry the Plantain
  8. Serve both together with a chilled drink. Enjoy

Jollof Rice

List of ingredients

  • Rice
  • Blended Pepper
  • Tomato Paste
  • Thyme & Curry
  • Chicken stock
  • Onions
  • Groundnut oil
  • Seasoning & Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat some oil, then add your grinded pepper
  2. Add your seasoning, salt, curry and thyme
  3. Allow it fry for 15 minutes
  4. Rinse your rice and pour into the pepper mixture
  5. Add the chicken stock and let it boil for 20 minutes
  6. Taste to see if more salt, seasoning is needed
  7. Leave rice to boil till its cooked(max 20 more mins)
  8. Serve with Chicken/Turkey etc and chilled drink. Enjoy!
